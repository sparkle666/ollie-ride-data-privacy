Request Account and Data Deletion for Ollie Ride

Your privacy is important to us. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from Ollie Ride, please follow the steps below.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Log into the Ollie Ride app.
  2. Navigate to Settings > Account.
  3. Select Delete Account and follow the on-screen instructions.
  4. Confirm your request by entering your password and tapping Confirm Deletion.

Data We Collect and Delete

When you request to delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Email Address
  • Password
  • Phone Number
  • Full Name
  • Location Data

No data is retained after the deletion request is processed.
